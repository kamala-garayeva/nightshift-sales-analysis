# Night Shift Sales Analysis 🌙

## Objective
Analyze total sales revenue generated during night hours (23:00–06:00) across store locations to understand off-peak performance and staffing efficiency.

## Dataset
Time-stamped transaction data from multiple retail stores, with sales amounts and store identifiers.

## Methods
- Filtered transactions to the 23:00–06:00 time window
- Aggregated total revenue by store during night hours
- Compared the night shift contribution to the overall daily revenue
- Identified the highest and lowest performing stores during this period

## Key Finding
Night shift revenue varies considerably by store location. Some stores generate substantial off-peak revenue, justifying extended operations, while others may benefit from reduced hours or staffing adjustments.

## Tools & Technologies
- **Language:** R
- **Libraries:** dplyr, lubridate, ggplot2
- **Analysis type:** Time-based revenue analysis
